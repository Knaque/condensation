condensation - ephemeral note-taking.

ephemeral (ephem·er·al)
1. lasting a very short time


write whatever you like in here.

it's never saved anywhere.

in fact, when this page is left/closed/refreshed, whatever you write is lost forever.

maybe you could write some hippie poetry or something, just like that one editor with no backspace key.

    drops on a mirror
    writing on condensation
    soon to disappear

or not, it's up to you.

after all, it's only temporary.
